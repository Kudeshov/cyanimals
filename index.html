About the project

This project documents and organizes information about animal welfare issues across Cyprus.

Through documentation, research, data collection, and public awareness, it highlights animal welfare issues, supports volunteers and caregivers, and promotes practical solutions for the protection of animals.

The project brings together information on animal welfare, community initiatives, rescue efforts, and documented cases of cruelty. By collecting reliable data and sharing evidence-based information, it aims to support better policies, stronger protection measures, and more effective cooperation between volunteers, organisations, local authorities, and the public.
